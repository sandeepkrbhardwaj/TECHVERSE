SkillSyncers Research on the Protégé Effect (2025):

This was our team’s very first research project, focused on the concept of the Protégé Effect, which means “learning by teaching.” We wanted to observe whether students who taught a topic to someone else would learn and remember the material better themselves.

To test this, we conducted our sessions on November 15 and November 16, 2025.

In the experiment, participants were first trained to teach a specific subject. After their teaching session, we tested them to measure how much their understanding and performance improved. Our initial findings clearly showed that explaining a concept to others significantly boosts the teacher’s own knowledge.

This research demonstrates that encouraging students to teach is a powerful and effective way to enhance their learning.
