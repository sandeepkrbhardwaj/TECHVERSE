SkillSyncers Research on the Protégé Effect (2025)

This was our team’s very first research project, focused on the concept of the Protégé Effect, which means “learning by teaching.”

To test this, we conducted our sessions on November 15 and November 16, 2025.

Participants were trained to teach a specific subject, then tested after teaching. Results clearly showed that explaining concepts significantly boosts understanding.

This research proves that encouraging students to teach is a powerful learning strategy.
